About Us

At Ember & Oak, we blend classic mixology with modern flair. Our bartenders craft each drink with seasonal ingredients, while our kitchen serves elevated small plates meant for sharing.

Whether it’s a date night or a late-night rendezvous, our warm lighting, curated music, and attentive service set the tone.

  • Signature cocktails
  • Hand-picked wines
  • Local craft beers
  • Small plates & tapas

Reception & Private Events

Planning an intimate reception, birthday, or team night? Our lounge area and high-top bar tables can be arranged for groups, with custom cocktail lists and shared plates to match your vibe.

  • Semi-private lounge for up to 30 guests
  • Customizable cocktail and food packages
  • Dedicated host and bartender
  • Ambient lighting and curated playlists
